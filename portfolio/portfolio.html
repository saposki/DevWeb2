Hirezippy

Hirezippy is in the forefront bridging the chasm between developers and with great talent and employers building the next generation of products and services. The platform provides powerful custom search engine that can scan across multiple parameters in location, interest, fitness and availability. It then provides a simple in app messaging system for users to communicate and make the deal.

In addition to all this it sports an intelligent matching system that dramatically reduces time to hire by alerting developers and employers of interesting prospects. Jobs are sourced from employers posting them directly and from selected partners verified to have quality jobs.
